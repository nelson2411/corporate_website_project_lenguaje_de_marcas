About Us

We are a community of League of Legends enthusiasts dedicated to helping players improve their skills and knowledge of the game. Our academy offers a variety of resources, including guides, tutorials, and coaching sessions, to help you become a better player.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive learning experience for League of Legends players of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced player seeking advanced strategies, we have something for you.

Services We Offer

Courses

Our courses cover a wide range of topics, including champion guides, item builds, runes, and game modes. Each course is designed to help you understand the fundamentals of the game and improve your gameplay.

Boosts

For a decent fee, one playe of our academy will play with you in ranked games to help you improve your skills and climb the ranks. Our boosters are experienced players who know the ins and outs of the game.

Guides

Our guides provide in-depth information on champions, items, runes, and game modes. Each guide is designed to help you understand the strengths and weaknesses of each champion and how to build them effectively.

News

Stay up to date with the latest news and updates from the League of Legends community. We provide regular updates on patch notes, champion changes, and upcoming events.
